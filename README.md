# book-tender

A utility for digitally storing your library, tagging books you read, taking pics of books you plan to read later.

## Description

book-tender is a digital library management tool that helps you organize and track your personal book collection. Whether you've read a book, are currently reading, or planning to read it later, book-tender makes it easy to catalog and manage your library digitally.

## Features

- **Digital Library Storage**: Keep a digital record of all your books
- **Book Tagging**: Tag and categorize books you've read
- **Photo Capture**: Take photos of books you plan to read later
- **Easy Organization**: Manage your reading list efficiently
